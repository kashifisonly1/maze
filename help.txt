		--- HELP MENU ---
Directions:
	NORTH: UP
	SOUTH: DOWN
	WEST: RIGHT
	EAST: LEFT
Commands:
	1. move N|S|W|E => to move to specified direction.
	2. look N|S|W|E => to change direction.
	3. get => to pick any key, diamond, or gems.
	4. open => to open door.
	5. examine => to open box.
	6. unlock => to open locked doors, you are require keys to open.
	7. inventory => to view inventory items.
	8. talk => to talk humans.
	9. save => to save your game.
	10. exit => to quit game.
