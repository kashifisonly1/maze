			--- STORY DESCIRPTION ---

This is an adventour maze game.
Game is based on the following story.
An inspector is got kidnapped by the enemies and is locked in an house.
Kidnappers are in other rooms and inspector can run away from the house.
There are gems and diamonds in the house that inspector can earn as reward.
If inspector succeeded to reach the end, you will win. If he enters the
kidnapper's room then you will lose.
Help the inspector to run away.
